🌿 KataleyaPrivacy-first architecture meets genuine empathy.Kataleya is a recovery companion designed to treat users with dignity. Moving away from the clinical coldness of medical trackers and the patronizing nature of gamified apps, Kataleya offers a quiet, intentional space for healing. Every technical decision—from its local behavioral engine to its multi-vault storage—is built on a single conviction: The app should know as little about you as possible.Key Features • Architecture • Circadian Engine • Installation✨ Key FeaturesThe GhostPulseOrb: A non-intrusive, visual grounding element that provides a sense of presence without monitoring.Garden Language: A progression system that uses organic, metaphorical growth rather than rigid clinical metrics.Golden Hour Transitions: UI shifts that mirror the natural world, easing the cognitive load during high-stress periods.The Burn Ritual: A digital-first approach to catharsis, allowing users to release thoughts into a secure, non-recoverable state.🛡️ Privacy ArchitectureKataleya is built on a Zero-Knowledge foundation. We don't just promise privacy; we architect for it.The Three-Vault SystemVaultPurposeSecurityIdentityBasic preferences and local configuration.Biometric-locked KeychainHistoryEncrypted logs of the journey.AES-256 (Local Only)The VoidEphemeral thoughts for the Burn Ritual.RAM-only / Auto-wipeBlind Relay MessagingCommunication within the ecosystem utilizes a blind relay protocol. Metadata is stripped at the edge, ensuring that even if a packet is intercepted, it contains no identifying behavioral markers or timestamps that could be linked back to a specific user.🌙 The Circadian EngineThe most novel aspect of Kataleya is its awareness of the human condition. A person in recovery at 2:00 AM faces different psychological hurdles than they do at dawn.The Circadian Engine is a locally-processed behavioral model that:Adapts UI Density: Reduces visual noise and blue light during late-night hours.Shifts Tone: Adjusts the "Garden Language" to be more grounding during "high-vulnerability" windows.Local-Only Nuance: No behavioral data ever leaves the device. The engine learns your rhythm without reporting it to a cloud.🛠️ Technical StackKataleya is engineered for resilience and absolute privacy.Core: [Rust/Swift/Kotlin] for memory safety and performance.Database: SQLCipher for transparent 256-bit AES encryption of the Three-Vault system.Visuals: Metal/Vulkan-driven shaders for the GhostPulseOrb and Golden Hour transitions.Logic: A custom, local-first state machine that powers the Circadian Engine.🤝 Philosophy & Contribution"Kataleya is what you get when privacy-first architecture meets genuine empathy for the person holding the phone."We welcome contributors who understand that software is a form of care.
