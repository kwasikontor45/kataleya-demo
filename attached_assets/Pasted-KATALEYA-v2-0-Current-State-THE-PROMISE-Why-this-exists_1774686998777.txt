THE THREE PILLARS: EXPLAINED SIMPLY
Pillar 1: Time Lives Here
Most apps: Check clock → set theme. Kataleya: Breathes with the sun.
Table
Time	Phase	What the app does	Why it matters
5:00 AM	Dawn	Gentle warm-up colors	Cortisol awakening response—don't shock the system
8:00 AM	Day	Full Morning Bloom	Peak alertness, productivity support
5:00 PM	Golden Hour	Amber→plum crossfade	Highest relapse risk. Protective color psychology.
8:00 PM	Night	Deep Midnight Garden	Melatonin production, sleep preparation
The technical trick: During Golden Hour, the app polls every 15 seconds (not 1 minute) and interpolates colors smoothly. No jarring switches. A sunset in your pocket.
